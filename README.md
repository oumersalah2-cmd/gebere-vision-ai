## What is it?

Gebere Vision AI is a live Telegram bot that helps Ethiopian smallholder farmers diagnose crop diseases instantly. A farmer sends a photo of their crop and receives a full diagnosis and treatment plan in **Amharic** or **Afaan Oromo** in under 30 seconds — completely free.

## Try it now

👉 **[t.me/gebere_vision_bot](https://t.me/gebere_vision_bot)**

## The problem

Millions of Ethiopian smallholder farmers lose up to 30% of their coffee and wheat harvests to preventable diseases every year. Agricultural extension workers are severely understaffed. By the time human help arrives, crops are already devastated.

## The solution

A Telegram bot powered by AI vision that acts as a digital agronomist:

1. Farmer sends a crop photo on Telegram
2. Bot analyzes the photo using Groq AI vision
3. Full diagnosis returned in Amharic or Afaan Oromo in under 30 seconds

## Features

- 📸 AI vision crop disease detection
- 🌍 Bilingual — Amharic and Afaan Oromo
- 💊 Instant treatment recommendations using local Ethiopian solutions
- 📊 Real-time data stored in Supabase PostgreSQL
- 🔄 Language preference saved per farmer
- 👍 Feedback system after every diagnosis
- ☁️ Deployed 24/7 on Railway cloud
